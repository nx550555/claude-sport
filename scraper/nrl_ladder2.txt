The NRL ladder shows the current standings for all teams in the National Rugby League competition. Teams earn two competition points for a win and one point for a draw, with ladder position determined by total points and points differential.

The ladder is updated after every match throughout the 2026 NRL season, showing each club’s wins, losses, points for and against, and overall differential. The top eight teams at the end of the regular season qualify for the NRL finals series and compete for the premiership.
